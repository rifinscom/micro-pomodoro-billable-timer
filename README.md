# Micro Pomodoro & Billable Timer ⏳

A minimalist, always-on-top timer designed for freelancers and digital nomads. Manage your focus sessions and track your work hours for client billing without the bloat of heavy web-based apps.

## 🔥 Features
- **Pomodoro Mode:** Pre-set 25-minute focus timer with a notification alert when time is up.
- **Stopwatch Mode:** Track your "billable hours" accurately for freelance projects.
- **Minimalist Design:** A tiny, floating window that stays on top of your work but doesn't take up much screen space.
- **Drag-Anywhere:** Move the timer anywhere on your screen with a simple click-and-drag on the background.

## ⚙️ How to Use
1. Install [AutoHotkey v2.0](https://www.autohotkey.com/).
2. Download `MicroPomodoroTimer.ahk`.
3. Select your mode (Pomodoro or Stopwatch) and start being productive!
